About Us

Kennesaw residents & KSU students—your new go-to laundromat is here! Opened in April 2024, we’re conveniently located at Frey Rd & George Busbee Pkwy (inside the Chevron plaza). Enjoy brand-new washers & dryers, plus flexible payments: credit cards, mobile apps, cash, and coins. Loyalty discounts available! Wash & Fold service started February 2025. Stop by and let us make laundry day easy!

Our Services

  • Accept Credit cards, Cash or Coins
  • Download Laundry Boss app to earn credits toward free washes and dryer time. Also, pay and start machine with app.
