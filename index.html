About Us

We are excited to announce the opening of this brand new coin laundry store in April 2024! The store is equipped with all new, state-of-the-art machines and located next to Kennesaw State University. We are committed to providing our customers with the best possible laundry experience, and we are confident that our brand new equipments and friendly staff will exceed your expectations. Wash & Fold services will be offered soon. We are conveniently located in the corner of Frey Rd and George Busbee Pkwy inside the Chevron shopping plaza. Come see why we are Washing away the Competition!

  • Download app to earn free washes and pay/contol machines with your phone and receive notifications
  • Wash & Fold (coming soon)
