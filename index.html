About Us

We’re thrilled to announce our brand-new coin laundry store opened in April 2024! Equipped with state-of-the-art brand new washers and dryers, we’re conveniently located next to KSU. Find us at the corner of Frey Rd and George Busbee Pkwy inside the Chevron shopping plaza. Come experience the difference!
